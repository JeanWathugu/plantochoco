TESTIMONIALS

If you don't trust us, hear personal testimonies from our farmers and loyal customers!

This is Lilly Wesonga.
She's a local cocoa farmer based in Kakamega county.
Western parts of Kenya provide the perfect environmental conditions required for the growth of cocoa.

"Not only has Plant-o-choco supported me financially via farm inputs,
they have also deployed an extension officer to make sure my plants were up to standard. For that I am forever grateful"



This is James Omanga.
He is an extension officer working for the company. He is a certified agricultural officer and has been in the industry for ten years now

"It's been a delight working with these local farmers; improving their farming techniques for a better output. Plant-o-choco has greatly invested in the local farming community. This is how you build a sustainable business."



This is May Wanjiku.
She's been a customer since the beginning. She has a dairy allergy that doesn't allow her to consume any dairy.
She wrote to us via email.

"Your almond based chocolates are my absolute fave. Especially on those bad days. It's a dairy alternative that actually tastes good. I love it!"
