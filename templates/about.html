About Us

Plant-o-choco is a plant based chocolate company, here in the heart of Nairobi. It was founded 10 years ago. We are catered towards every sweet tooth who can not consume dairy for one reason or another. We provide an alternative to dairy chocolate while still maintaining flavour and pleasure! We have a variety of chocolate flavours made using almond, coconut and soy milk.
We also work with local cocoa farmers as a way to empower the local community and Kenya's economy in general!
